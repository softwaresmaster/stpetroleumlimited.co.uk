About ST Petroleum Limited

Who we are

ST Petroleum Limited operates the local Gulf-branded forecourt and convenience store at Wainhouse Corner Garage. We're dedicated to dependable fuel, friendly service and a well-stocked convenience outlet for the local community and visitors to Bude and surrounding areas.

Our mission

To deliver excellent customer service, quality fuel and convenient products while supporting local partners and maintaining high standards of safety and responsibility.

History

Our family-run garage has served the community for many years. We focus on consistent quality and helpful staff, operating in partnership with Gulf UK.

What we offer

  • Gulf-branded fuel
  • Convenience store (snacks & drinks)
  • Calor Gas, ATM, Off Licence
  • PayPoint and delivery partners
